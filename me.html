MY AUTOBIOGRAPHY

WRITTEN BY: Trisha Agaba

WHO AM I?

My name is Trisha Agaba Esther. I am 20 years old. I am a Ugandan, to be specific, a Munyankole. I am a very beautiful, confident and independent woman with so much life in me. I have a very beautiful smile that you can't fail to see when you meet me. It's a smile that would brighten your day and completely change your mood. I am very loving and charming with eyes for days. I am currently doing software engineering at Makerere University which I love so much. I think choosing this course was one of the best decisions I made and i don't regret any secong of it. I am very Godfearing. I believe in the existence of a higher power and I think this world is a beautiful place and life is a beautiful thing that we take for granted.
My course is a four year course whereby I've only moved about a quarter into it. We learn different coding languages such as Java, Javascript, C++ among others. My course requires a lot of concentration and dedication whereby you have to read ahead of the lecturer if your goal is to ace the course.

A LITTLE BIT ABOUT MY FAMILY

I am the third born in a family of four.I have two sisters and one brother. My sisters' names are Trinah and Tracey and my brother's name is Tristan. Each of my siblings is unique in their own way. My family gives me so much joy and is currently the most important thing in my life.
